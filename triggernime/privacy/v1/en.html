This Privacy Policy describes the procedure for processing personal data of users of the Telegram bot (hereinafter referred to as the “Bot”). By using the Bot, the user gives consent to the processing of personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Personal Data Processed

Within the operation of the Bot, the following personal data may be processed:

  • unique Telegram user identifier (Telegram User ID);
  • displayed username (nickname);
  • selected interface language;
  • gender specified by the user;
  • date and time of registration and last activity;
  • date and time of acceptance of user terms;
  • purchase history of digital content within the Bot;
  • information about bonuses and rewards accrued for invited users and their purchases.

We do not request or store passport data, residential addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, or bank card details.

2. Purposes of Data Processing

Personal data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • ensuring the operation and functionality of the Bot;
  • user identification within the system;
  • operation of game mechanics and personal settings;
  • providing access to purchased content;
  • accounting of purchases, bonuses, and rewards;
  • ensuring security and service stability.

3. Messages and Media

The content of user messages is not stored as chat history and is used only for processing commands and in-game interactions.

Media files (images, audio, video, voice messages) sent by the user are used exclusively within the current game session and are not stored after the interaction is completed.

4. Payments and Data Transfer

Payment data is processed by payment service providers. Bank card details and other payment credentials are not stored on the Bot’s servers.

We do not transfer personal data to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes. Data transfer is possible only in cases provided by law and to the minimum extent necessary for the operation of payment services.

5. Data Storage and Protection

The Operator applies reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, modification, or deletion.

6. Data Retention Period

Personal data is stored for the duration of the use of the Bot or until deletion at the user’s request, unless otherwise required by the legislation of the Russian Federation.

7. User Rights

The user has the right to:

  • receive information about processed personal data;
  • request correction or deletion of personal data;
  • withdraw consent to the processing of personal data;
  • stop using the Bot at any time.

To delete personal data, the user may contact the Operator using the contact information provided above.

8. Age Restriction

The Bot is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. By using the Bot, the user confirms that they are at least 18 years old.

9. Policy Changes

The current version of the Privacy Policy is always available via a public link. Continued use of the Bot signifies the user’s consent to the updated version of the Policy.
