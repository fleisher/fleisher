Personal data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

The content of user messages is not stored as chat history and is used solely for command processing and in-game interaction.

Payment transactions are processed through third-party payment services. Bank card details and other payment information are not stored on the Bot’s servers.

Personal data is not transferred to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes. Data may be transferred only in cases provided by the legislation of the Russian Federation and to the extent necessary for the operation of payment services.

The Data Controller applies reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

To exercise these rights, the user may contact the Data Controller using the contact details provided above.
